## 2. Headline Engine Logic (Live Feed) 
### A. The 1-Card Template (Rising Demand) 
**Logic**: Triggered when the first card is placed in an empty segment.
* **Template**: `[Voter Segment] Headline: "Demands for [Interest Bloc] begin to rise among [Segment Citizens]."` 

### B. The 2-Card Template (The Tense Alliance) 
**Logic**: Triggered when a second, different color is added.
* **Template**: `[Voter Segment] Headline: "A tense alliance forms as [Bloc A] and [Bloc B] interests collide in [Segment]."` 

### C. The 3-Card Payoff (Irony Logic) 
**Logic**: When a segment reaches 3 cards, the engine pulls a unique irony-heavy headline based on the specific combination.
